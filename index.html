About Me

I am very passionate in keeping up with the advance pace of frontend technology. Whether it is creating applications or just a simple website, there is always something new to learn. One of the best feelings for me is when I turn something complicated into something simple. Growing up, I have always love to take things apart and fix it up again. It's like my father always say, "You can't learn how things function unless you break it." Those are one of the life lessons he has taught me, and I hold it with every day.

Anyone that knows me personally knows that when I am not coding, I like to play fighting games. I am a very big in the FGC (Fighting Game Community) and attend local events. Although I do not go to tournaments like how I would like to, I give full support in the community. Besides that, I love to spend time with my family.
